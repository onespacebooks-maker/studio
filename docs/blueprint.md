# **App Name**: CuraNet: Healthcare Connection

## Core Features:

- Appointment Booking: Real-time booking with doctors and hospitals.
- Service Price Comparison: Compare treatment costs across hospitals with insurance details.
- AI Symptom Guide: Suggest the right medical department based on user-described symptoms, using a form or chatbot as a reasoning tool.
- Teleconsultation: Integrated low-cost video call scheduler.
- Medical EMI: Enable splitting medical bills into monthly payments during checkout.
- Health Wallet: Track daily micro-savings for healthcare expenses.
- Family Health Dashboard: Manage family health appointments, records, and reminders.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Royal Blue (#4169E1) to convey trust and sophistication.
- Background color: Light desaturated blue (#D1DFF5) to create a calm and clean feel.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) to evoke a sense of elegance and royalty.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif with an elegant feel; body text: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif for readability. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Simple, clear icons to improve usability and understanding.
- Clean, spacious layout with a prominent hero section featuring friendly illustrations of diverse Indian families.
- Subtle hover effects and button transitions to enhance user engagement without overwhelming the user.
